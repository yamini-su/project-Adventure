SINGAPORE

UNIVERSAL STUDIOS - SENTOSA GATEWAY

Known as “one of its kind theme park in Asia,” the Universal Studios Singapore speaks for its 24 attractions located within 7 theme areas that include Hollywood, New York, Sci-Fi City, Far Far Away, Madagascar, The Lost World, and Ancient Egypt.


WHAT'S SO SPECIAL ABOUT IT?

Meet your favorite Hollywood stars and even dine with them, watch jaw-dropping stunts and sci-fi shows, or chase the dinosaurs from the Jurassic Park.

BEST TIME TO HAVE FUN

The best time to visit the Studio is all around the year. However, you should keep in mind that weekends are the busiest when tourists in great number throng to the attraction. Also, March, June, September, and December are the busiest months due to school holidays.


ITALY

GARDALAND PARK

Gardaland is the Number 1 Amusement park in Italy, the one that has been around the longest in Italy and is one of the most important at European level.


WHAT'S SO SPECIAL ABOUT IT?

Embark on a fantastic adventure with the themed-water well, and the thrilling rides that includes Blue Tornado, Raptor, Magic Mountain, Sequoia Adventure, Oblivion – The Black Hole, and a lot more.

BEST TIME TO HAVE FUN

The best time to visit Lake Garda is the summer(July - August), when temperatures rarely fall below 25°C.


UNITED KINGDOM

ALTON TOWERS - ENGLAND

Opened in 1980, Alton Towers in UK is one of the top amusement parks in the world that consist of a theme park, water park, and a hotel complex along with treatment rooms in Staffordshire, England.


WHAT'S SO SPECIAL ABOUT IT?

Embark on an unforgettable journey of fun and musings at the tropical Caribbean themes, the paradise plumbers, the Master blaster water coaster, and the exhilarating rides only at Alton Towers.

BEST TIME TO HAVE FUN

Alton Towers Theme Park in United Kingdom is open from March until November every year. Wander around theme park in summer to experience best fun.


FRANCE

DISNEYLAND - PARIS

Disneyland Paris is one of the classical attractions that is known to be among the well-known and biggest amusement parks in the world. Disneyland offers entertainment complexes, musical tour of world attractions, roller coaster rides, shopping, and much more.


WHAT'S SO SPECIAL ABOUT IT?

The incredible themes like Big Thunder Mountain, and Pirates of the Caribbean are just a few of the numerous surprises that must not be missed when you enter into the most sought theme parks in all of Europe.

BEST TIME TO HAVE FUN

If you want to visit when the number of Guests is fairly low, plan to stay mid-week (Tuesdays to Thursdays) during mid-January through mid-March or mid-April through mid-May.


AMERICA

UNIVERSAL ORLANDO RESORT

Opened on June 7, 1990, the park's theme is the entertainment industry, in particular movies and television.The park encourages its guests to "ride the movies" and features numerous attractions and live shows.


WHAT'S SO SPECIAL ABOUT IT?

Feel the highs and lows on Harry Potter, Transformers, Revenge of the Mummy, Men in Black Alien Attack, Kang & Kodos' Twirl 'n' Hurl, E.T. Adventure, Despicable Me Minion Mayhem and The Simpsons-themed rides.

BEST TIME TO HAVE FUN

April and May are some of the best months to visit Universal Studios. This is the 'shoulder season' when you'll get the best of peak season and low season perks.
